My SP Experience!

These are some snippets from my current Singapore Poly Life! Take some time to browse around and take a look at the different images!

Classrooms in SP!


As you can see, these are the types of classrooms present in SP! They very much differ from the single tables and chairs from secondary school, and was quite a nice change. Fitted with air-conditioning and charging ports, these classrooms are equipped for the use of our laptops. To be honest, almost every studying table present in SP has a charging port to allow its students to charge their electronic devices such as laptops and mobile phones.

Hilltop Haven!


Ah yes, Hilltop. Being in Block T22, the closest 'library', as many would consider it, would be Hilltop Haven. Equipped with an in-house cafe and freezing air-conditioning temperatures, many students would consider the multi-storey building a haven to study in. See what I did there? My personal experience with Hilltop is rather positive, as my friends and I would head off to Hilltop after classes to finish our homework and collaborate on group projects.
